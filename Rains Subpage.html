How to sub

Subscribe in the thread in T&T and post a 🍟 in it. In fact, any word or emoji within 3 letters will be hidden to others, but you can still get notified by comments in there.

That means you can keep it short and simple while still getting the benefit of updates. If you were not sure how to do it before, this is the quick version and it only takes a moment.
